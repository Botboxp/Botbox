About us

WHO WE ARE

Botbox Productions is an award-winning film production and post-production creative studio, specializing in digital media advertising.

We are a team of talented artists with backgrounds in a variety of disciplines from fine art, videography, photography and design.

We have built entertainment brands for television networks, films and advertising agencies, receiving recognition through various Academy of Television Arts & Sciences awards (Emmy Awards).
