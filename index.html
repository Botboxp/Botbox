How we work

OUR PROCESS

01

BRIEF & CONCEPT

We listen to your vision, understand your brand and define the creative goals. Every production starts with a story.

02

PRE-PRODUCTION

We plan every detail: locations, casting, technical team, storyboard and logistics. Nothing is left to chance.

03

PRODUCTION

We capture images with state-of-the-art cinematic equipment. Art direction, lighting and first-class camera operation.

04

POST & DELIVERY

Editing, color grading, visual effects and audio mixing. We deliver in all formats you need, ready for broadcast.
